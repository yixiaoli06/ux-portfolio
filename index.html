My Work

Fitness Recipe Mobile App Design

Recifit is a conceptual mobile application that encompasses both fitness and healthy diets by highlighting personalization on nutritional needs. Per our research, it is currently rare to find a dietary assistance service that takes a user’s BMI, nutritional needs, and taste preferences into consideration through the recommendation of recipes. With this idea in mind, we as a group of three UX design students wish to create a prototype for this concept that potentially helps accommodate the user’s fitness goals and lifestyle.

Non-Profit Website Redesign

World Parks Inc is a non-profit organization that aims to achieve the preservation and the expansion of national parks on a global level. Its mission is to raise awareness and to fundraise for the goal of global environmental conservation, which I am personally enthusiastic about. I, along with 3 cohorts of my project team noticed that its current website is in need of a more thoughtful information architecture and higher quality visual elements. The plethora of information on this website is not organized intuitively, which causes the organization to appear unprofessional. Therefore, we as UX design students see an opportunity to help improve its current website to better integrate with the organization's mission and purpose.
